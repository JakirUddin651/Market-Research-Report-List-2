<p><ul><li>Waist Harness</li><li>Chest Harness</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Windsurfing harnesses are essential for connecting the sailor to the sail, allowing for better control and reduced fatigue. Waist harnesses sit around the waist, providing mobility and comfort, making them popular for recreational windsurfers. In contrast, chest harnesses wrap around the chest, offering superior support and load distribution, ideal for high-performance sailing and maneuverability. The choice between these two types depends on the rider's style, skill level, and preference for comfort versus support in different wind conditions.</p></p>
