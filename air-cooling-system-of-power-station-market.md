<p><strong>The Air Cooling System of Power Station Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Air Cooling System</li><li>Indirect Air Cooling System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air cooling system in power stations comprises two main types: direct and indirect air cooling systems. Direct air cooling systems utilize ambient air to cool the condensers directly, improving efficiency in areas with sufficient air availability. In contrast, indirect air cooling systems use heat exchangers to transfer heat from the condensers to the air, which can be beneficial in regions where air temperature is high. Both systems aim to enhance thermal efficiency while minimizing water usage in energy generation processes.</p></p>
