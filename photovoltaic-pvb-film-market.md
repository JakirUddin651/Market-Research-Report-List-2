<p><ul><li>Transparent</li><li>Opaque</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photovoltaic PVB (polyvinyl butyral) film market is segmented into two main types: transparent and opaque. Transparent PVB films are used primarily in solar panels to enhance light transmission while providing structural support and protection, making them ideal for building-integrated photovoltaics. Opaque PVB films, on the other hand, are used in applications where light blocking is necessary, such as in certain architectural installations or shading solutions. Both types play a critical role in improving energy efficiency and optimizing solar energy conversion.</p></p>
