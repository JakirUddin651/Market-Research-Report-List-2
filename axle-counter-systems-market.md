<p><ul><li>Rail Side Installation</li><li>On-rail Installation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Axle Counter Systems are essential for monitoring train movements and ensuring safety on railway networks. The market is divided into two main types: Rail Side Installation and On-Rail Installation. Rail Side Installation involves placing axle counters alongside the track to detect the passage of trains, providing data from a fixed location. On-Rail Installation, on the other hand, integrates the axle counters directly onto the train itself, allowing for real-time monitoring and enhanced accuracy in train tracking and operations.</p></p>
