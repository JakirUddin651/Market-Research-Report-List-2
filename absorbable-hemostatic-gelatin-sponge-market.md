Skin gelatin sponges, derived from animal skin, are utilized for controlling bleeding in soft tissue surgeries due to their flexibility and biocompatibility. Bone gelatin sponges, created from animal bones, are designed for orthopedic applications and effectively promote blood clotting and tissue regeneration in bony areas. Both types are absorbable, providing essential hemostatic properties while being gradually integrated into the body.</p></p>
