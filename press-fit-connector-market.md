<p><p>A press fit connector is a type of electrical connector designed to be inserted into a printed circuit board (PCB) without the need for soldering. This method uses interference fit technology, whereby pins are pressed into holes in the PCB, creating a reliable electrical and mechanical connection. The advantages of press fit connectors include reduced assembly time, lower manufacturing costs, and improved durability compared to soldered connections. </p><p>The Press Fit Connector Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for compact and efficient electronic components across various applications such as telecommunications, automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial machinery.
