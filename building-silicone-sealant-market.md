<p><ul><li>One Component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The building silicone sealant market is primarily divided into two types: one-component and two-component systems. One-component sealants are ready-to-use products that cure upon exposure to moisture in the air, making them convenient for various applications. In contrast, two-component sealants require the mixing of a base and a hardener before application, allowing for customized properties and enhanced performance, especially in demanding environments. Each type caters to specific needs in construction and building maintenance, influencing their market dynamics.</p></p>
