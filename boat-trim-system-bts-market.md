<p><ul><li>Trim Tabs</li><li>Interceptors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Boat Trim System (BTS) market comprises various technologies used to enhance vessel performance and stability. Key types include trim tabs and interceptors. Trim tabs are adjustable surfaces mounted on the transom that optimize the boat's angle and reduce drag, improving fuel efficiency and handling. Interceptors are more advanced devices that offer rapid trim adjustments, enhancing maneuverability and stability at high speeds. Both technologies contribute to better control, comfort, and overall boating experience, appealing to recreational and commercial users alike.</p></p>
