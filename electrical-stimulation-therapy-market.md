Magnetic Field Therapy uses magnetic fields for cellular healing, while Ultrasound Therapy employs sound waves to promote tissue repair. Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation (TENS) Therapy sends electrical impulses through the skin to reduce pain perception.
