<p><p>Wound Healing Assay Kits are vital tools for assessing cell migration and proliferation during the wound healing process. The market for these kits includes two primary detection methods: colorimetric detection and fluorometric detection. </p><p>Colorimetric detection measures changes in color intensity, providing quantitative data that reflects cell migration. Conversely, fluorometric detection utilizes fluorescent tags to visualize and quantify cellular activity, often offering greater sensitivity and dynamic range. Both methods cater to varying research needs in cellular biology and regenerative medicine.</p></p>
