<p><p>The electrical fittings market is divided into two main types: metallic and non-metallic fittings. Metallic electrical fittings, made from materials like steel and aluminum, offer durability, high-temperature resistance, and superior conductivity, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, non-metallic electrical fittings, typically crafted from PVC, fiberglass, or thermoplastic materials, provide corrosion resistance, lightweight properties, and enhanced insulation, making them suitable for residential use and environments that require moisture resistance. Both types play crucial roles in electrical installations.</p></p>
