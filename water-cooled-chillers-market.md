<p><p>Water-cooled chillers are essential for industrial and commercial cooling needs and come in various types. Centrifugal chillers use high-speed rotors for efficient cooling in large applications. Scroll chillers rely on spiral mechanisms, making them suitable for smaller spaces with lower cooling demands. Screw chillers utilize helical rotors for continuous flow and high efficiency, ideal for medium to large operations. Other market types may include absorption chillers, used in specific applications where waste heat is available, enhancing overall energy efficiency.</p></p>
