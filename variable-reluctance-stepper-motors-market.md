<p><p>Variable Reluctance Stepper Motors (VRSMs) are a type of stepper motor that operates on the principle of varying magnetic reluctance. These motors consist of a stator with multiple coils and a rotor that is often not magnetized but instead has a toothed structure. When electrical current flows through the stator coils, a magnetic field is produced, causing the rotor to align with the field, leading to precise angular movement.</p><p>The Variable Reluctance Stepper Motors Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increased demand for automation in various industries such as manufacturing, robotics, and automotive. As industries seek more efficient and accurate motion control solutions, VRSMs are gaining popularity due to their reliability and cost-effectiveness.
