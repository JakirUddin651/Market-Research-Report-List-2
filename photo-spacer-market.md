<p><ul><li>Negative Type</li><li>Positive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photo spacer market is categorized into negative and positive types based on their functionality in imaging processes. Negative type photo spacers are used primarily in applications requiring light blockage, such as in photography or optical devices, ensuring that specific areas are shielded from light exposure. Conversely, positive type photo spacers facilitate light passage and are essential in applications like projection systems, where enhancing light transmission and clarity is crucial. Both types play vital roles in optimizing imaging performance across various technologies.</p></p>
