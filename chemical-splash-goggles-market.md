<p><p>The chemical splash goggles market features two primary types: non-vented and indirect vented goggles. Non-vented chemical splash goggles provide a sealed environment to protect against hazardous liquids, preventing any splashes from entering. They are ideal for environments with high contamination risk. Indirect vented chemical splash goggles, on the other hand, allow for airflow to reduce fogging while still offering protection against splashes. This design strikes a balance between safety and visibility, making them suitable for a wider range of applications.</p></p>
