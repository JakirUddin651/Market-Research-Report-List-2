<p><p>The gynecological examination lamp market comprises three main types: LED, halogen, and fluorescent lamps. LED lamps are energy-efficient, providing bright, adjustable light while minimizing heat output, enhancing patient comfort. Halogen lamps offer intense illumination and better color rendering, but generate more heat. Fluorescent lamps are cost-effective and widely used; however, they may not provide the same level of brightness or clarity as LED and halogen options. Each type serves distinct needs in clinical settings, influencing choice based on performance and operational cost.</p></p>
