<p><p>The polymer-based solid-state battery market comprises two main types: all-solid-state batteries and semi-solid batteries. All-solid-state batteries utilize a solid polymer electrolyte throughout, offering enhanced safety, energy density, and stability. In contrast, semi-solid batteries combine solid and liquid components, achieving a balance between performance and manufacturability. Both types aim to improve energy storage solutions for electric vehicles, consumer electronics, and renewable energy systems, addressing the limitations of conventional lithium-ion batteries and paving the way for next-generation energy technologies.</p></p>
