<p><p>The stainless steel pipes and tubes market is primarily categorized into seamless and welded types. Seamless pipes are manufactured without joints, offering higher strength and durability, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. In contrast, welded pipes and tubes are created by joining metal pieces together, which makes them more cost-effective and suitable for medium-pressure applications. Both types find extensive use in industries such as oil and gas, construction, automotive, and food processing, driven by their corrosion resistance and versatility.</p></p>
