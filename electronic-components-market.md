<p><p>The electronic components market is categorized into three main types: active components, passive components, and electromechanical components. Active components, such as transistors and integrated circuits, can amplify and control electric signals. Passive components, like resistors and capacitors, do not generate energy but store or manage it. Electromechanical components, including relays and switches, combine electrical and mechanical functions, facilitating the control of electrical circuits. Together, these components form the foundation of modern electronic systems and devices.</p></p>
