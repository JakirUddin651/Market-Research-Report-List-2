<p><p>The prescription hearing aid market consists of various types designed for user comfort and functionality. In-the-ear (ITE) hearing aids fit directly in the ear, offering a discreet option. Behind-the-ear (BTE) aids rest behind the ear, providing enhanced power and battery life. In-the-canal (ITC) aids are smaller, fitting inside the ear canal for a more subtle appearance. Other types may include receiver-in-canal (RIC) or bone-anchored devices, catering to diverse hearing loss needs and preferences.</p></p>
