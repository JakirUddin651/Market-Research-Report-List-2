<p><p>Shrink sleeve films are classified into several types based on material characteristics. PETG (Polyethylene Terephthalate Glycol) offers clarity and eco-friendliness, appealing to sustainable packaging trends. OPS (Oriented Polypropylene) provides excellent graphics and is lighter, enhancing cost efficiency. PLA (Polylactic Acid) is a biodegradable option, catering to environmentally conscious consumers. Each type serves distinct market needs, balancing performance, sustainability, and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
